### Step 1
Go to your [billing account](https://billing.vexyhost.com/user/security). This link will redirect to you Security Settings page.

### Step 2
Click **Two-Factor Authentication** tab, then click on **Click here to Enable** green button.

![](https://i.imgur.com/yG3RR1E.png)

### Step 3
We recommend using **Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator**, just **scan the generated QR and enter the 6 digits** of the Google Authenticator.

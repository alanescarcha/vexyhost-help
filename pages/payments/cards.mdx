## Argentina - ARS
To make credit and debit card payments from Argentina in Argentine Pesos (ARS).

<Callout>
For credit card payments in Argentina, we use Mercado Pago. You can use Debit or Credit Cards. **You don't need a Mercado Pago account to pay**
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Step 1 - Find the invoice
Go to [your invoices](https://billing.vexyhost.com/clientarea.php?action=invoices). Find the invoice you want to pay and click on the invoice.

### Step 2 - Select payment method
There, you should choose the option **Mercado Pago - ARS (Solo Argentina - Only Argentina)** and then click on the Pay button.

![](https://i.imgur.com/fIn2PPK.png)

Then you will be redirected to Mercado Pago. You must choose whether to pay by debit or credit card. If you pay by credit card, you will be able to pay with two cards.

![](https://i.imgur.com/aGtsQQE.png)

### Step 3 - Payment

Finally, you should click on "Pagar" button.

![](https://i.imgur.com/sTh6YlN.png)

</Steps>

## International - USD
To make credit or debit card payments from any other country in US Dollars (USD).

<Steps>

### Step 1 - Find the invoice
Go to [your invoices](https://billing.vexyhost.com/clientarea.php?action=invoices). Find the invoice you want to pay and click on the invoice.

### Step 2 - Select payment method
There, you should choose the option **Other Global Payments - Otros metodos globales** and then click on the Pay button.

![](https://i.imgur.com/bitysUg.png)

### Step 3 - Your details
You will then be redirected to the Tebex Checkout payment gateway. A form will be displayed that you need to fill out with your billing information.

![](https://i.imgur.com/wJ7uuvo.png)

### Step 4 - Payment
Afterwards, depending on your country, different payment methods will appear as shown in the screenshot below. You should choose one of them and provide the necessary information.


<Callout type="info">
All countries have the option to pay with cards.
</Callout>

![](https://i.imgur.com/RL7mSRT.png)

</Steps>

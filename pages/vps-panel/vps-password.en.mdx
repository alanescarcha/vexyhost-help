# Change VPS Password

import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

<Callout>
If you forgot the password of your VPS, either Windows or Linux this guide can help. **Remember to use strong passwords.**
</Callout>

## Linux
Go to your [VPS panel](https://vps.vexyhost.com/). Go to `Settings` and then on the `Change Password` tab.
![](https://i.imgur.com/FpFb6Li.png)

<Callout type="info">
You may need to **restart the VPS from the panel** for the new password to be set. To learn how to reboot your VPS from the panel follow [this article](/vps-panel/start-vps).
</Callout>

## Windows
Go to your [VPS panel](https://vps.vexyhost.com/). Go to `Settings` and then on the `Change Password` tab.
<Callout type="warning">
Leave the `Windows Admin Name` field blank to be able to change the Administrator password.
</Callout>
![](https://i.imgur.com/7QKEgpp.png)

<Callout type="info">
You may need to **restart the VPS from the panel** for the new password to be set. To learn how to reboot your VPS from the panel follow [this article](/vps-panel/start-vps).
</Callout>
